Senior Manager & Head of IT at RightWave, overseeing enterprise infrastructure across three locations — US Data Center, Noida DC+Office, and Dehradun branch. I manage Dell PowerEdge/VMware clusters, Windows/Linux VMs, databases (MS SQL, MongoDB, MySQL, Redis), web servers (Tomcat, Apache, Nginx, WordPress), email deliverability (MX/SPF/DKIM/DMARC), pfSense firewalls, ISO 27001/ISMS compliance, and DNS/SSL end-to-end.
